Project Overview

This Industrial Data Acquisition & Signal Conditioning System is designed to capture, process, and analyze analog and digital signals from industrial equipment. The system uses precision signal conditioning circuits and high-resolution ADC modules to ensure accurate measurement of voltage, current, and sensor outputs. It enables real-time monitoring, noise filtering, and reliable data transmission for industrial diagnostics and process optimization.
