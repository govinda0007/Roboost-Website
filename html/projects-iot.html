Smart connected solutions for industrial monitoring and automation.

This Industrial Energy Monitoring System enables real-time tracking of voltage, current, power, and energy consumption of industrial equipment. The system uses ESP32 with a PZEM-004T energy module to collect electrical parameters and transmit data securely to a cloud dashboard. It helps industries analyze power usage, detect overload conditions, and reduce energy wastage through intelligent monitoring.
